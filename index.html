The beginning

In 1993, Daft Punk created and released a demo tape and met Stuart MacMillan at a EuroDisney rave. They released their first single, titled "Alive," which helped them gain success. Their 1995 single, "Da Funk," was their first national and worldwide hit. Their 1997 debut album, Homework, was well-viewed and incorporated the single "Around the World".



The ending

Starting in 1999, Daft Punk began performing in public wearing helmets, gloves, and outfits to disguise their identities. Collaboration with The Weeknd In 2016, Daft Punk collaborated with The Weeknd on the songs "Starboy" and "I Feel It Coming". They performed both songs at the 2017 Grammy Awards, which was their final performance together. Breakup In 2021, Daft Punk sadly announced their breakup on YouTube.
